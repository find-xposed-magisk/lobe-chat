# Upcoming Changes to WeChat Chat

Since WeChat chat launched, we have received a great deal of usage and feedback from users — thank you for your continued support.

We would like to share some context: WeChat chat relies on an external WeChat message channel to send and receive messages. Due to that channel's communication mechanism, stability requirements, and ongoing operating costs, the long-term maintenance cost of WeChat messaging is significantly higher than that of regular chat channels.

To keep the service stable and make WeChat chat sustainable over the long term, we are making the following changes:

- Users who have already connected WeChat can continue to use it as usual during the notice period, without any impact.
- After the notice period ends, creating a new WeChat connection, re-binding WeChat, or continuing to use WeChat chat will require upgrading to a subscription plan.

This change applies only to the WeChat message channel. It does not affect LobeHub's web chat, model usage, or other core features.

If you would like to keep the convenience of WeChat chat, we recommend completing your subscription upgrade before the notice period ends. Thank you for your understanding and support.
